# What/Why is DevThink.AI

*By Sam Keen on May 23, 2023*

---

Are you a software developer seeking to stay on the cutting edge of AI tools and trends? My goal is to make this the perfect resource for you.

We understand the constant evolution of AI tools can be overwhelming. Thats why were here to sift through the noise and bring you the most pertinent and practical information. Each issue will feature the latest AI tools, detailed tutorials, insightful use-cases, and news on industry trends.

The goal is to make [DevThink.AI](http://devthink.ai/) more than just a newsletter. Its an opportunity to be part of a community of like-minded developers, all committed to leveraging AI to enhance their efficiency and code quality.
